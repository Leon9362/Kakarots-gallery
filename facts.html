Facts about Siberian Cats

  • Siberian cats are a natural breed from Russia.
  • They have a triple-layered, water-resistant coat.
  • Siberians are known for being hypoallergenic, producing less of the Fel d 1 allergen.
  • They are playful and are often described as "dog-like" in their loyalty.
  • These cats are powerful jumpers and love to climb.
  • Siberians are slow to mature, taking up to five years to reach full size.
